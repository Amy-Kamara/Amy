All about London

London is, without a doubt, one of the greatest cities in the world!

With over 8 million residents and being one of the most visited destinations in Europe, there are so many reasons why this city is simply magical.

Why I love London

  1. It has a rich cultural history
  2. You can find food and cuisine from all over the world
  3. An abundance of museums, galleries and experiences to be had at at price point
  4. Its becoming increasingly cycle friendly
  5. Lots to do with kids, or at any age!
  6. In the summer, its a vibe!

Why you should visit

  • Cool castles and palaces
  • Easy to navigate by train or bus or walking
  • Lots to do, all year round
